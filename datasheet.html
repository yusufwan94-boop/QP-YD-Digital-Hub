Why Saturation Voltage (Vo)?

The saturation voltage represents the Stopping Potential. In a solar cell, as we increase light intensity (Vin), the output voltage (Vo) eventually plateaus. This "plateau" is our true data point because it indicates that the maximum kinetic energy of the electrons has been reached.
